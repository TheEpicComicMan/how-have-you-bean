Daily Observations



Date: 3.1.18

Today the beans arrived home. The first thing to do is to soak them. I let the beans soak in warm water for around 24 hours. They are to be pulled out tomorrow.


Date: 3.2.18

The beans were taken out of the water. They were taken out of the water and placed on a damp paper towel in a Ziploc bag.


Date: 3.3.18

Nothing much to report today, they were in the Ziploc bag for the entire day.


Date: 3.4.18

Nothing much to report today, they were in the Ziploc bag for the entire day.


Date: 3.5.18

Today is the day the beans get removed from the bag. Having germinated, they were planted about one inch deep in potting soil.


Date: 3.6.18

Today the beans were watered and placed out in the sun. I think I over-watered them as the pot looks like a swamp.


Date: 3.7.18

The beans are left out overnight and I have seen no growth since yesterday, not that I expected any.


Date: 3.8.18

No growth and the pot still is soaked in water even hough I have not re-watered the plans.


Date: 3.9.18

I got a new pot for the plants today. In fact, a new pot for each bean. I feel they will grow much better now that they do not have to fight for resources.


Date: 3.10.18

Still no growth from any of the beans, I'm starting to get worried. I think soon I will move them inside and give one constant light and water.


Date: 3.11.18

More water and sun today, still no growth.
